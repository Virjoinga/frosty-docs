PVZCharacterWeaponData

Description

Contains stuff for PvZ Weapons

Events

  • OnChargingStarted
    • When weapon charging has begun.
  • OnChargingStopped
    • When weapon charging has stopped.
  • OnDisabled
    • Something, idk.
  • OnPlayerActivate
    • When weapon is active.
  • OnPlayerDeactivate
    • When weapon is inactive
  • OnFiringEnded
    • When firing has stopped
  • OnFiringStarted
    • When firing has begun.
  • onPrimaryFireProjectileSpawned
    • When the projectile of the primary weapon has spawned.
  • OnReloadBegin
    • When reloading the weapon has begun.
